What Sets It Apart From Diamond And Pearl?

Heres a list that details some key differences Pokemon Platinum holds to Pokemon Diamond and Pearl

  • Firstly the main characters for this game are suited with new outfits for the colder weather
  • Team Galactic has a new leader that goes by the name Charon
  • Team Galactics new sinister plot includes all the legendary Pokemon from the creation trio: Giratina, Dialgia and Palkia
  • Pokemon Platinum adds a new NPC, a detective by the name of Looker who will help you on your adventures throughout the game
  • You start the game by watching an interview being conducted with Prof.Rowan
  • You meet the Prof right when you attempt to leave town on route 201
  • After choosing your starter pokemon (Chimchar, Piplup, or Turtwig) your rival will challenge you right away
  • Cyrus, the previous leader of team Galactic in Pokemon Diamond and Pearl stands before the main character overlooking lake Verity
  • The Sinnoh Pokedex in Pokemon platinum has an additional 51 pokemon, making the new Sinnoh pokedex total 210 pokemon
  • Giratina now has its new form, first seen in its newly added domain, the distortion world (players final battle with Cyrus will also take place here.)
  • Giratina also was given new stat changes along with its new form
  • The mythical pokemon Shaymin was also given a new form called sky form. A special item called the gracidea flower is needed to obtain this new form
  • After talking to Prof.Oak and gaining the national pokedex the legendary bird trio will also be availble for capture around the Sinnoh region, Zapados, Arcticuno and Moltres
  • When exploring Hearthome city the player will encounter Bebe, a creator of the pokemon PC system. After talking with her she will give you a lvl 20 Evee, a highly coveted pokemon
  • In veilstone city an NPC will also offer the player a Porygon
  • In the resort area the player can own a house and choose to decorate it with a multitude of decor options
  • The back sprites of Pokemon have now been animated, something highly requested in the commmunity during this time
  • In some of the poke centres wandering trainers will battle you if you engage in conversation with them, unlike other trainers these trainers can be challenged daily
  • The Pokemon Rotom (found in the haunted house) has new forms based on different electrical appliances (a fan, a fridge, a oven and a lawnmower

The Battle Frontier And Wifi Plaza!

Pokemon Fire Red, Leaf Green, and Emerald versions battle frontier has been added to Pokemon Platinum to add even more for players to enjoy! The new battle frontier includes numerous areas for players to challenge each other with unique rules and new NPC's that run the battle frontier named the Frontier Brains. The other addition alongside the battle frontier was the Wifi Plaza, located in the basement in Poke Centres (take the stairs down). The wifi plaza was added to give players a way of communicatinf/exchanging information while in game. The Wifi Plaza is an amusement park that provides players with a number of mini games to play along with other players, up to 20 players can be in a single session and they can connect from anywhere in the world. The wifi Plaza doesn;t just have games but also parades and fireworks!

Gym Leaders And their Pokemon!

The Rock Type Gym Leader Roark

Roark is the first Gym leader you battle. Meant as an introduction to the gym system, he's a relatively easy opponent. His pokemon are down below.

  • Geodude, lvl 12
  • Onyx lvl 12
  • Craindos lvl 12

The Grass Type Gym Leader Gardenia

Gardenia, the 2nd gym leader you challenge poses more of a threat then Roark. Still an early game gym leader so not too much of a challenge. Her Pokemon are down below.

  • Turtwig lvl 20
  • Roserade lvl 20
  • Cherrim lvl 20

The Ghost type Gym Leader Fantina

Fantina being a ghost type pokemon trainer uses status affects to battle, giving the trainer a harder time. Her Pokemon are down below.

  • Duskull lvl 24
  • Haunter lvl 24
  • Mismagius lvl 26

The Fighting Type Gym Leader Maylene

Maylene uses fighting typ pokemon, relying on heavy hitting attacks and speed to win. Her Pokemon are down below.

  • Meditite lvl 28
  • Machoke lvl 29
  • Lucario lvl 32

The Water Type Gym Leader Crasher Wake

Crasher Wake is a menacing gym leader, using powerful water type pokemon to overpower his foes. His Pokemon are down below.

  • Gyrados lvl 33
  • Quagsire lvl 34
  • Floatzel lvl 37

The Steel Type Gym Leader Byron

Byron uses defense heavy steel type pokemon, making having a fighting type pokemon a near necessity. His Pokemon are down below.

  • Magneton lvl 37
  • Steelix lvl 38
  • Bastiodon lvl 41

The Ice Type Gym Leader Candice

Candice uses Ice type pokemon to freeze her opponents solid, make sure to carry a frost heal or two! Her Pokemon are down below.

  • Sneasel lvl 40
  • Pillowswine lvl 40
  • Abomasnow lvl 42
  • Froslass lvl 44

The Electric Type Gym Leader Volkner

Volkner, the final gym leader the player challenges is a true trainer, his pokemon are well trained and a true threat. His Pokemon are down below.

  • Jolteon lvl 46
  • Raichu lvl 46
  • Luxuray lvl 48
  • Electivire lvl 50
