What Sets It Apart From Diamond And Pearl?

Heres a list that details some key differences Pokemon Platinum hold to Pokemon Diamond and Pearl

  • Firstly the main characters for this game are suited with new outfits for the colder weather
  • Team Galactic has a new leader that goes by the name Charon
  • Team Galactics new sinister plot includes all the legendary Pokemon from the creation trio: Giratina, Dialgia and Palkia
  • Pokemon Platinum adds a new NPC, a detective by the name of Looker who will help you on your adventures throughout the game
  • You start the game by watching an interview being conducted with Prof.Rowan
  • You meet the Prof right when you attempt to leave town on route 201
